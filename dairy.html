Dairy

Dairy mostly comtains calcium which helps in the maintainence of your bones. Dairy products include food items such as yogurt, cheese, and butter. Dairy products are high in potassium, comparable to potassium-rich foods. As an electrolyte, adequate potassium is crucial for your muscles and nerve cells to work properly; the Linus Pauling Institute reports that a diet high in potassium may also lower your risk of high blood pressure, which can help prevent heart disease.
